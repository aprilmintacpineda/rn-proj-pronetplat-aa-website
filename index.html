We believe that privacy is not optional, but networking requires that you give up some of your privacy to your contacts/network. So we are striving to create an ecosystem where people can keep in touch with or build their network with the guarantee that the tool they are using values their privacy.

What Entrepic allows you to do

NOTE: Pictures of people and their accounts used here are ficticious and only used for demonstration purposes.

Quickly add other users to your network by scanning their QR code. It works even when you're offline! Provides a really good alternative to business cards.

They will receive a contact request.



Once accepted, you will see each other in your corresponding contact list.

And you will be able to view each other's contact details.
