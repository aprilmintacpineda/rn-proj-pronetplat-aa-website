Abstract

This document describes what data the system collects, how they are being used by the system, and to whom those data are being shared to. Changes to this document may be done upon our discretion. We will notify you of any changes in this document through the email address you used to register to the system. If your account has been inactive for 3 months or more, you may not receive any further email of the said notifications.

Personally identifying data

Definition

These are data that can be used to identify your personal identity

Collection

These data are all entered by you through forms that you fill-in and submit using the mobile app and other forms that this website links to. Example of these forms are register form, personal information form, feedback form.

Inclusion

These may include but are not limited to the following:

  • Your email address that you used to create an account.
  • Your full name.
  • Your gender.
  • Your job title.
  • The name Company you work for
  • Your picture that you use as profile picture.
  • Contact detail that you added such as mobile, telephone, website, or email.

Usage

We do not, in any way, use these data internally. These data are what are required for you to be able to use the system in the way that it was intended to be used.

We use your email to send you notifications about your account and other system related updates. These notifications include but are not limited to:

  • Your account's password changed.
  • Request to reset your password was triggered.
  • Email verification upon creation of your account.
  • Changes in our privacy policies.
  • Changes in our Terms and conditions of use.

Analytics data

Definition

These are anonymous data that we collect from you while using the application.

Collection

We collect these data as on the background as you use the application. These data cannot be used to identify your personal identity.

Inclusion

These data include but are not limited to:

  • Your location which we derive from your IP address.
  • Your app usage, such as screens you view.

Usage

We use these data to internally observe how you, our customers, use the application so that we can make he right efforts to improve the service that we offer you through the system so that the system may better serve.
